How It Works

Our QR code generator is designed to be simple, fast, and privacy-focused. Here's how to create your custom QR code:

Step 1: Enter Your URL

Type or paste the website address you want to encode into the QR code. Make sure to include "https://" for web URLs.

Step 2: Customize Your Design

Choose from various styles (classic, dots, rounded, gradient) and select a color that matches your brand or preference.

Step 3: Add a Logo (Optional)

Upload a logo or image to place in the center of your QR code. This helps with branding and makes your QR code unique.

Step 4: Generate and Download

Click the generate button and within seconds your custom QR code will be ready. Download it as PNG or SVG for use in your projects.

Privacy First

All processing happens directly in your browser - no data is sent to our servers. Your URLs remain private and secure.

About QRGen

QRGen is a free, privacy-focused QR code generator that works entirely in your browser. We believe in creating useful tools that respect user privacy and work seamlessly across all devices.

Our Mission

To provide a fast, reliable, and secure way to generate QR codes without compromising user privacy. We don't store your data or track your activity.

Features

  • 100% client-side processing (no server requests)
  • Multiple QR code styles and customization options
  • Logo upload capability
  • PNG and SVG download options
  • Works offline after initial load
  • Mobile-friendly interface
  • Dark mode support

Technology

QRGen uses modern web technologies including HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. QR code generation is handled by the trusted qrcode.js library.
